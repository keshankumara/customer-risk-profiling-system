# Customer Risk Profiling System

This repository contains the Customer Risk Profiling System, a project that helps organizations understand and monitor customer risk in a clear, consistent way. It brings together risk signals and presents them in a unified view so teams can spot issues early, prioritize investigations, and make informed decisions.

## What This Project Covers

- A complete system view that ties together risk analysis, reporting, and decision support.
- A user experience focused on risk monitoring, customer review, and alert handling.
- A foundation for future expansion into automated policies and deeper analytics.

## Who It Is For

- Risk and compliance teams who need a reliable picture of customer risk.
- Fraud analysts who need to review customers and alerts efficiently.
- Stakeholders who want visibility into how risk is assessed and tracked.
